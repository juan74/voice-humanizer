# CORPUS.example.md — Template for Your Own Voice Corpus

This is what your CORPUS.md should look like. Delete this file and rename your version to CORPUS.md, or replace CORPUS.md directly.

**CORPUS.md is in .gitignore** — it doesn't get pushed to the public repo. Other people who clone this skill bring their own writing. You're not sharing your corpus, just the structure.

---

## How to Build Your Corpus

Paste 3–5 pieces of writing you're proud of. Not polished-but-generic pieces — pieces where you felt like yourself. A post where the voice felt right. A comment thread where you were sharp. An email where you said exactly what you meant.

The skill derives your voice fingerprint from these. More is better up to about 5 pieces. After that, length matters more than count.

### What to include

- Full published articles or posts (best)
- Long-form comment exchanges you're proud of (good)
- Technical documentation you wrote in your own voice (fine)
- Emails or Slack messages that capture how you think (fine for calibration)

### What to skip

- Pieces you wrote under deadline pressure and didn't love
- Pieces where you were being careful to sound "professional" rather than yourself
- Anything written partly by AI
